About Us

Promethean is a Web site for artists and designers by artists and designers. Our goal is to push art and graphic design to new levels by showing you the newest design styles out there, and giving you the inside tips on how they are accomplished. Promethean covers ad design, magazine design, Web design, poster design and all styles of art from all mediums.

The idea of Promethean began in the beginning of 2010, when four students from the Art Institute of Pittsburgh decided to bring their talents from all areas of media to create a Web site for artists and designers.

In 2011 that idea became a reality when Promethean was launched on January 1, 2011. The content of Promethean includes critiques on the latest artists and styles, galleries, contests and tutorials about the latest techniques.
